Who Has Believed

A Gospel Tract Based on Isaiah 53

About This Tract

This powerful gospel tract explores the profound message of salvation found in Isaiah 53. Through careful exposition of this messianic prophecy, readers are led to understand the suffering servant's role in our redemption and the importance of believing in Jesus Christ.

The tract presents the gospel message clearly and compassionately, making it perfect for personal evangelism, church outreach programs, and sharing with friends and family who need to hear the good news.

Key Scripture Focus

"Who has believed our message and to whom has the arm of the Lord been revealed?" - Isaiah 53:1

What's Inside

  • • Clear explanation of Isaiah 53's messianic prophecy
  • • The significance of Christ's suffering for our salvation
  • • Personal application and call to faith
  • • Scripture references throughout
  • • Gospel presentation suitable for all audiences

📋 Complete Printing & Assembly Guide

1

Review First

Download and review the Standard PDF to ensure the pages are in the correct order and the content reads properly.

2

Print Setup

Use the Print-Ready (Imposed) version with these printer settings:

  • • Quality 8.5" × 11" paper
  • • "Fit to printable area"
  • • "Print on both sides" (duplex)
  • • "Flip on long edge"
  • • "Collate" enabled
3

Cut, Fold & Staple

After printing:

  • • Cut along the guidelines to separate booklets
  • • Fold each booklet in half along the center line
  • • Staple in the center fold to secure pages

💡 Pro Tip: The imposed version arranges pages so that when folded and stapled, the tract reads in the correct order. Always use the standard PDF first to verify content before printing multiple copies.
