Complete Printing & Assembly Guide

1

Review First

Download and review the Standard PDF to ensure the pages are in the correct order and the content reads properly.

2

Print Setup

Use the Print-Ready (Imposed) version with these printer settings:

  • • Quality 8.5" × 11" paper
  • • "Fit to printable area"
  • • "Print on both sides" (duplex)
  • • "Flip on long edge"
  • • "Collate" enabled
3

Cut, Fold & Staple

After printing (2 whole sheets make one booklet):

  • • Cut the page in half (short ways) carefully
  • • Trim along the cut marks
  • • You should now have 4 long pieces of paper
  • • Use the normal PDF to check the order and stack the 4 pieces in the correct order
  • • Fold the stacked pieces in half along the center
  • • Staple in the center fold using saddle staple style

Pro Tip: The imposed version arranges pages so that when folded and stapled, the tract reads in the correct order. Always use the standard PDF first to verify content before printing multiple copies.
