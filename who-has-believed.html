Who Has Believed?

An Ancient Story of Suffering That Impacts You Today

About This Tract

This thoughtfully written tract shares the gospel through one of the most powerful prophecies in the Old Testament—Isaiah 53. Written centuries before Christ's birth, this ancient text provides remarkable detail about Jesus' sacrificial death and resurrection.

The tract carefully shows how Isaiah's prophecy was precisely fulfilled in Jesus Christ, addressing the central question: "Who has believed our report?" Download it for free to read the complete presentation of the gospel through this amazing Old Testament prophecy.

Gospel Through Prophecy

This tract demonstrates how Isaiah 53's detailed prophecies about the "Suffering Servant" were fulfilled in Jesus Christ:

  • • His rejection and suffering
  • • Why He bore "our transgressions" and "our iniquities"
  • • His death and resurrection
  • • How to receive this gift of salvation

Complete Printing & Assembly Guide

1

Review First

Download and review the Standard PDF to ensure the pages are in the correct order and the content reads properly.

2

Print Setup

Use the Print-Ready (Imposed) version with these printer settings:

  • • Quality 8.5" × 11" paper
  • • "Fit to printable area"
  • • "Print on both sides" (duplex)
  • • "Flip on long edge"
  • • "Collate" enabled
3

Cut, Fold & Staple

After printing:

  • • Cut along the guidelines to separate booklets
  • • Fold each booklet in half along the center line
  • • Staple in the center fold to secure pages

Pro Tip: The imposed version arranges pages so that when folded and stapled, the tract reads in the correct order. Always use the standard PDF first to verify content before printing multiple copies.
